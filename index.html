Mental Shortcuts

Rule of 2 and 4

Quick estimation of draw completion odds

Flop → River (2 cards to come)
Outs × 4 ≈ %
Turn → River (1 card to come)
Outs × 2 ≈ %

Slightly overestimates for high out counts (>9 outs). Exact formula: 1 − (miss/remaining) × (miss−1)/(remaining−1)

Multi-way Pots

Equity drops sharply with more opponents. A hand with 50% equity heads-up might have only 30% against two opponents and 20% against three. Always factor in the number of players when deciding to call.

A quick sanity check: divide your heads-up equity estimate by the number of opponents to get a rough multi-way figure.
